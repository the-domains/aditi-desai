I am Aditi Desai, living in Stockholm. A biotechnologist with a Business degree, born and brought up in India I represent a mix of cultures, traditions, geographies, languages and ideas. The idea of travelling always occupied a significant portion of my brain but I could never realise it until I took off for my first travel after I moved to Stockholm. And after then it has just been a journey to discovering a new me every time I travelled, a calling !
